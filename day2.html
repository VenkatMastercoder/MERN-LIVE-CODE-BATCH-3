Dosa is not just symbolic of South India

A trip to any South Indian state is incomplete without having a taste of possibly the most quintessential food of the area - dosa. Dosa is not just symbolic of South India, but it also doubles as one of the many foods that constitute the Indian cuisine. Of course, no place in India can replicate the authentic and traditional taste of dosa like South Indian states can, and Chennai is a city that is no stranger to providing the most mentally and gastronomically wholesome dosas. A big city where dosa is a staple, it is hard to shortlist restaurants that are the best at dosa-making.
